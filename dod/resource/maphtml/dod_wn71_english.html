Mission Brief D-Day, 6 June, 1944
The Allies have crossed the English Channel and are about to storm the beaches of Normandy while the Wehrmacht plan to defend the coast of France by any means necessary.

U.S. Army:
Storm the beach, breach the beachhead fortifications and capture the 'Forward Bunker' to allow the Allies to advance their starting position. You must then capture 3 subsequent objectives to win - 'Rear Bunker', 'Crater' and 'Radio Command' within the time limit.
  
Wehrmacht:
Defend the beachhead at all costs, you will have 8 Minutes to stop the allied advance. Once the Allies have captured the 'Forward Bunker' their starting position will move forward and the Wehrmacht starting posiiton will move deeper into the bunkers.
Notes:
  • Explosives can be set by the Allies at 2 locations along the shingle - Infront of the doors of the Forward Bunker and on the Barbed Wire to the left of the Forward Bunker.
  • The Allies must capture the Forward Bunker in order to advance their starting location.
  • There are 2 routes from the Beachhead to the Bluffs to the right and left of the Forward Bunker.
  • Once the Allies capture the Forward Bunker the Axis starting position moves back and they will no longer be able to enter the Beachhead.
  • Capturing the Forward Bunker will add 30 seconds to the timer.
  • The Axis cannot enter the Beach.
  • The Axis cannot capture the Forward Bunker.
  • All capture points require 2 People to capture.
